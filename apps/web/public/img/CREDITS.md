## The set

Raw high-res sources are committed under `apps/web/scripts/photos-src/`; the build
script `apps/web/scripts/build-photos.mjs` applies one consistent warm grade and
emits pre-sized WebP + AVIF (two widths) into `apps/web/public/img/`. To reproduce:
`node apps/web/scripts/build-photos.mjs`.
